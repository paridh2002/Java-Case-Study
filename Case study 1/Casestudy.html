About the College

Symbiosis Institute of Technology (SIT), Nagpur, established in 2020 under Symbiosis International (Deemed University), aims to deliver world-class technical education with a focus on innovation and entrepreneurship.

The campus is located in Nagpur's lush green surroundings, equipped with state-of-the-art laboratories, smart classrooms, and an innovation hub. Courses include B.Tech in CSE, ECE, AI/ML, and more.

The institution emphasizes holistic development through academic excellence, cultural diversity, and global collaboration. Students are mentored by highly qualified faculty and participate in international exchange programs, hackathons, and research projects.
